About Us

Genshin Impact takes place in the fantasy world of Teyvat, which is home to seven distinct nations,
each of which is tied to an element and ruled by a god associated with it. The story follows a twin,
referred to as the Traveler, who has traveled across many other worlds but has become separated
from their twin sibling in Teyvat. The Traveler travels in search of the lost sibling with their
companion Paimon and becomes involved in the affairs of Teyvat's nations and gods.
